About Us

Founded in 1973, the Institute of Theoretical Pigeonology (ITP) is dedicated to the study of pigeon behavior in urban warfare, interspecies diplomacy, and the hidden symbology of cooing patterns.

Our Mission

To decode the language of pigeons and uncover their influence on human civilization. From their role in ancient espionage to their modern urban governance, pigeons have shaped history in ways unseen.
